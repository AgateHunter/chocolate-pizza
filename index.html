For the fig swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to disolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one of two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.
